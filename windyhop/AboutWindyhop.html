About Windyhop...

So, are you wondering what Windyhop is all about? Well, if so, then you've come to the right place! Windyhop is more than just a website. Windyhop is a group of people in Chicago who love Swing music and dancing, and want to share that love with the rest of the world. We will strive to provide fantastic Swing-related information that may not be readily available elsewhere. We have built this website with you in mind, and we hope you enjoy it.
