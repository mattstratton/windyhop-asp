National and International Swing Events

Looking for something exciting to do? There are many national and international Swing events that you could attend. These events range from camps and workshops to purely social activities. If you're interested in learning more, check out the links below!


EventDescriptionLocationDate
Swing Camp CatalinaSwing camp held o n the beautiful Catalina Island, located off the coast of California. Swing Camp Catalina features classes taught by some of the most respected instructors from all over the world.Catalina IslandJune 2000
Beantown 2000Swing camp which runs for two consecutive weeks during the summer. You can sign up for one week or both, depending on your stamina! Beantown features small class sizes which allows for some special attention from some of the greatest Swing instructors in the world today.Wheaton College - Boston, MAJuly 2000
Herrang Dance CampHerrang is a dance camp put on by the Rhythm Hot Shots. Herrang runs for four consecutive weeks during the summer. Dancers from all over the world meet up and have a great time here each year.Herrang, SwedenJuly 2000
